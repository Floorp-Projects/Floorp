MochiKit.LoggingPane

Demonstrates MochiKit's MochiKit.Logging and MochiKit.LoggingPane.

Click one of the Logging Panes to pop up a view, and then start clicking on Logging Actions to see it in action!

  • Inline LoggingPane
Logging Actions:
